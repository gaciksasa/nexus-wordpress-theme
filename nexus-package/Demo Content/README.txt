Nexus Demo Content
==================

All 15 demo sites are bundled directly within the theme and can be imported
via the built-in setup wizard.

After activating the Nexus theme, navigate to:

  Appearance > Nexus Setup

The wizard will guide you through plugin installation and demo content import.

Available Demos:

  1. Business            9. Education
  2. Creative Agency     10. Real Estate
  3. Restaurant          11. Gym & Fitness
  4. Fashion Shop        12. Travel
  5. Portfolio           13. Photography
  6. SaaS                14. Law Firm
  7. Medical             15. Startup
  8. Finance

Each demo imports:
  - Content (XML) - pages, posts, menus, and media placeholders
  - Widgets (WIE) - sidebar and footer widget configurations
  - Customizer (DAT) - theme colors, typography, and layout settings

Note: Demo images are placeholders only. Replace them with your own images
after import.
